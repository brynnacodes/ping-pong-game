Let's Play!

Rules:

Ping-pong will count up by whole numbers, from 1 to your entered number with the following exceptions:

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 become "ping"
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 become "pong"
  3. Numbers divisible by 15 become "ping-pong"
